What is word hunt?
Word Hunt is a mini-game released by GamePigeon, an iMessage extension. It features a 4x4 grid of letters. You and your opponent each have 1 minute and 20 seconds to find as many words as possible. To form a word, connect adjacent letters; however, each letter tile can only be used once per word. The player with the highest score at the end of the game wins!
Scoring System:
3 letter words: 100 points
4 letter words: 400 points
5 letter words: 800 points
6 letter words: 1400 points
7 letter words: 1800 points
8 letter words: 2200 points
9 letter words: 2600 points
10 letter words: 3000 points
